The Story Behind Sectional

Eleven years in choirs taught me one thing: rehearsal recordings are useless. The basses overpower the altos, the sopranos overpower everyone, you can't isolate your part, and by measure twelve, you've lost your place. I spent a year as an Isang Himig intern wrangling sheet music in MuseScore, another year leading the alto section - trying to teach music my section could barely hear on the reference track. Sectional is what happens when that singer finally learns Flask, Demucs, and the Claude API.
