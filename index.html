Sectional

A practice tool for choirs and a cappella groups.

Eleven years in choirs taught me one thing: rehearsal recordings are useless. The basses overpower the altos, the sopranos overpower everyone, you can't isolate your part, and by measure twelve, you've lost your place.

Sectional fixes that. Pick your voice part, type the line you're practicing, and get coaching tied to the score - no uploading required (there's a built-in demo score). If you add a recording, the app splits the mix and brings your part forward so you can actually hear yourself.

Under the hood: React/TypeScript frontend, Python/Flask backend with 11 REST API endpoints, Claude API for structured natural-language analysis, ChromaDB vector search for score-grounded retrieval (2.5s max query latency, 2,500-chunk scan cap), Demucs for audio separation, and SciPy for vocal emphasis. Deployed live on Render and AWS Lambda.

#Python #Flask #ClaudeAPI #Demucs #React #TypeScript

The Story Behind Sectional

Eleven years in choirs taught me one thing: rehearsal recordings are useless. The basses overpower the altos, the sopranos overpower everyone, you can't isolate your part, and by measure twelve, you've lost your place. I spent a year as an Isang Himig intern wrangling sheet music in MuseScore, another year leading the alto section - trying to teach music my section could barely hear on the reference track. Sectional is what happens when that singer finally learns Flask, Demucs, and the Claude API.
